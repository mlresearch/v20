Quadratic Weighted Automata:
Spectral Algorithm and Likelihood Maximization

R. BAILLY; JMLR W&CP 20:147–163, 2011.

Abstract

In this paper, we address the problem of non-parametric density estimation on a set of strings Σ∗. We introduce a probabilistic model – called quadratic weighted automaton, or QWA – and we present some methods which can be used in a density estimation task. A spectral analysis method leads to an eﬀective regularization and a consistent estimate of the parameters. We provide a set of theoretical results on the convergence of this method. Experiments show that the combination of this method with likelihood maximization may be an interesting alternative to the well-known Baum-Welch algorithm.
